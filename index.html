About Us

Established in 2006, Kema Energy Solutions, Inc. is a private firm engaging in the business of manufacturing and marketing of electrical and mechanical components and parts.

Our team is composed of professional managers, engineers, and support staff who deliver dependable service and technical expertise to every project. SOY MEI Advantage Multi Sales, Inc. serves as the parent company and the marketing arm.

Mission

Our mission is to be a trusted supplier of high-quality electrical supplies and materials by providing reliable products, competitive pricing, and exceptional customer service. We are committed to supporting residential, commercial, and industrial projects through timely delivery, technical expertise, and ethical business practices while building long-term partnerships with our customers and suppliers.

Vision

Our vision is to become the leading and most trusted supplier of electrical supplies and materials, recognized for quality products, exceptional customer service, and innovative solutions that empower homes, businesses, and industries.
