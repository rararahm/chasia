About Us

Established in 2006, CH Asia World of Electric, Inc. is a major distributor of Eaton and other global electrical brands in the Philippines.

Our team is composed of professional managers, engineers, and support staff who deliver dependable service and technical expertise to every project. SOY MEI Advantage Multi Sales, Inc. serves as the parent company and the marketing arm for Eaton products.

We have built strong business links with major developers and commercial establishments such as SM Group of Companies, Robinsons Land Corporation, Megaworld Corporation, Anchor Land Holdings, and ETON Properties Philippines.

Mission

To be visible among project developers, electrical designers, households, and other end users by providing quality products, continual support, and reliable delivery beyond client expectations.

Vision

To pursue progressive growth through quality projects, products, services, and methods that make problem solving faster, easier, and more dependable for every client.

Company History

2006

CH Asia World of Electric, Inc. was established and registered with the Securities and Exchange Commission.

May 2006

Signed a distribution agreement with former Eaton–Cutler Hammer, Singapore, expanding our role in Eaton brands such as Westinghouse, Cutler Hammer, Powerware, and more.

Present

Now recognized as a trusted partner with projects for major developers and an expanded portfolio including Schneider, ABB, LS, Shihlin, and Optimus Busduct.
